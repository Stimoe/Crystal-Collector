CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

You will be given a random number at the beggining of the game

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specidic amount of points to your total

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The valiue of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
